Sodium

Sodium is a chemical element with symbol Na (from Latin: natrium) and atomic number 11. It is a soft, silver-white, highly reactive metal. In the Periodic table it is in the alkali metals column (2nd-3rd row). It is highly reactive, oxidizing, and discolored in air.

Wikipedia

Silicon

Silicon is a chemical element with symbol Si and atomic number 14. It is a tetravalent metalloid, more reactive than germanium, the metalloid directly below it in the table. It does not occur naturally as a free element in nature, but is prepared in the mineral gadolinite (SiO2).

Sodium Chloride

Sodium chloride (NaCl) is a chemical compound with the formula NaCl. It is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, and highly inflammable, alkaline, highly reactive, and highly corrosive metal. It is the main constituent of sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO3), sodium chloride (NaCl), sodium hydroxide (NaOH), and sodium nitrate (NaNO3).

Sodium Hydroxide
